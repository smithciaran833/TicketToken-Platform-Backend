# On-Call Guide

## Rotation
- Weekly rotation, handoff Monday 9am
- Primary + Secondary on-call
- Escalation to platform team lead after 30 min

## Responsibilities
- Monitor #auth-alerts Slack channel
- Respond to PagerDuty within 15 min
- Follow runbooks in /docs/runbooks/

## Escalation Path
1. Primary On-Call (15 min)
2. Secondary On-Call (15 min)
3. Platform Team Lead
4. Engineering Manager

## Contact
- Slack: #auth-service, #auth-alerts
- PagerDuty: auth-service escalation policy

## Post-Incident
1. Create incident ticket
2. Write post-mortem within 48 hours
3. Schedule review meeting
4. Track action items to completion
